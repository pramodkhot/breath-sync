Privacy Policy

Breathe Easy — Guided Breathing App

Short version: Breathe Easy does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever. Everything stays on your device.

Effective date: June 11, 2026
Developer: Pramod Khot  |  pramodsk1214@gmail.com

1. Information We Collect

We collect no personal information. The app does not require an account, login, or any identifying details to function.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

Breathe Easy saves your custom breathing-timer preferences (inhale, hold, exhale, rest durations and round count) using Android's local storage (AsyncStorage). This data:

  • Never leaves your device.
  • Is not transmitted to any server or third party.
  • Is erased when you uninstall the app.

3. Internet & Network Access

Breathe Easy does not request internet permission and makes no network connections. The app works fully offline.

4. Third-Party SDKs & Analytics

The app contains no advertising SDKs, analytics libraries, crash-reporting services, or any other third-party components that access or transmit data.

5. Permissions

Breathe Easy requests no device permissions (no camera, microphone, location, contacts, storage, or notifications).

6. Children's Privacy

Breathe Easy is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update the effective date above and publish the new version at this URL. Because we collect no data, material changes are unlikely.
